These resources may be helpful for people impacted by or wanting information about the Caldor Fire in El Dorado County. If there is information missing, let me know and I will update this page.

Emergency Information

1. Sheriff official evacuation map https://eldoradocounty.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=c995bf3816964e948d7d831d3ba938ff
2. Enroll in El Dorado County CodeRED phone alerts: https://ready.edso.org/
3. El Dorado county added a resource page to their website with incident information and is continually updating it. https://www.edcgov.us/Pages/Caldor-Fire.aspx
4. Live scanner feed: https://www.broadcastify.com/webPlayer/35390

Other Information Sources

Twitter list with a few accounts which listen to the scanner and post what's important. They post about more than just the Caldor Fire. Hashtag is #CaldorFire. You do not a Twitter account to watch. https://twitter.com/i/lists/1427723826314371075
Local news website Yubanet has dedicated regional-fire page feed and was very helpful during the King fire in 2014. https://yubanet.com/regional-fires/caldor/
Incident summary and incident maps - El Dorado National Forest https://inciweb.nwcg.gov/incident/7801/ https://inciweb.nwcg.gov/incident/maps/7801
Map with imagery from MODIS and VIIRS instruments aboard orbiting satellites. Each dataset only updates 1-2 times daily as the satellite passes overhead. https://caltopo.com/map.html#ll=38.65509,-120.51659&z=12&b=mbt&a=modis_mp
